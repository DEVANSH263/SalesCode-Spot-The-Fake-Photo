# Spot the Fake Photo

Point your camera at something real, or at another screen/printout showing a
photo. The model scores every frame live:

- **0 = real photo**
- **1 = photo of a screen** (recapture / fraud)

Built for the SalesCode AI take-home — see the full write-up in `note.md`
in the [GitHub repo](https://github.com/).
